Welcome

FLITE (FGLI in Tech and Engineering) is a student organization that provides catered support, opportunities and development for FGLI (First-generation, Low-income) students at Penn passionate about technology and engineering to ensure success for a population with unique needs.

Get Involved

We will focus on providing a variety of professional and academic resources to share with other students, as well as partner with companies year round to provide career opportunities in technology and engineering. Sponsor today if you are a company and would like to help our mission, and get involved as a student by clicking on the link below.

MENTORSHIP

We aim to provide academic, professional, and general guidance for incoming students through our mentorship program that pairs upperclassmen with freshmen.

RESOURCES

We plan to partner with a variety organizations from technology companies to community non-profits to provide a wide-degree of academic and professional resources.

NETWORK

We hope to develop our students through interdisciplinary collaboration to create leading engineers that will create innovative solutions to a variety of society's problems.
