Committees

Penn FLITE is focused on providing resources to help promote success for first generation/low income technology and engineering students at Penn. We are open to eager students willing to help us out and are looking for areas of expertise below!

Web Design and Content Committee

Our website is constantly growing with new features and resources waiting to be implemented. Join us and help us realize these goals while developing your own design and development skills.

Communications and Outreach Committee

As a newer organization on campus, we will need to expand our outreach. This committee is tasked with marketing such as managing our Facebook page and creating flyers and banners.

Professional Development and Events Committee

We will need students to reach out to external groups and create events. This committee is tasked with assisting on internal events such as with setting up events and workshops for Penn students.
