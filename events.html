WiCS Collaboration Wev Development Workshop

02.26.2020

We had an amazing turnout for our Web Development workshop, which was planned and executed in collaboration with Women in Computer Science. Led by our FLITE co-founders Jesse Cui and Yeleine Pineda, we introduced an audience to the art of developing websites from scratch. After this workshop, attendees left with a general understanding of how to use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Git to build a personal site, with most being able to build their personal site during the workshop
