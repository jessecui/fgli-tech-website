WiCS Collaboration Wev Development Workshop

02.26.2020

We had an amazing turnout for our Web Development workshop, which was planned and executed in collaboration with Women in Computer Science. Led by our FLITE co-founders Jesse Cui and Yeleine Pineda, we introduced an audience to the art of developing websites from scratch. After this workshop, attendees left with a general understanding of how to use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Git to build a personal site, with most being able to build their personal site during the workshop

GBM 1 of Spring 2020

02.05.2020

We had a great turnout for this GBM. We started the night with some icebreakers, then discussed several opportunities and resources available to FGLI students in Tech and Engineering. Toyce Holmes, Pennâ€™s FGLI Program Coordinator, was in attendance. Among several things, she encouraged students to join and use Penn FLASH, a network developed by Penn First Plus Alumni and the FGLI program, for mentoring and career development purposes. After this, we discussed upcoming events and opportunities, which were not limited to: Web Development Bootcamps, CURF and CSAP events, and our Shared Resource Drive for FGLI students in Tech and Engineering.

Machine Learning Workshop

11.14.2019

This amazing Machine Learning Workshop was led by Jesse Cui, FLITE cofounder and CIS 520 TA. He explained essential concepts in Machine Learning, such as data set preprocessing and Python for data science. After the theoretical section, we coded along with him on a structured Google Colab. Attendees left understanding the fundamentals of Machine Learning and equipped with the tools to continue to do projects on their own.
