ABOUT US

About Us

Our Purpose

FLITE (FGLI in Tech and Engineering) is a student organization that provides catered support, opportunities and development for FGLI (First-generation, Low-income) students at Penn passionate about technology and engineering to ensure success for a population with unique needs.

Our Mission

We believe in fostering equal opportunity and support to help all students of all backgrounds fulfill their passions and overcome all barriers by providing resouces, mentorship, and a community for FGLI students in engineering to promote success and opportunity.
